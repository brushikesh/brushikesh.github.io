The Need of the hour

With cut-throat competition in virtually every sphere, be it a product industry or service industry, you will agree that catching the attention of the customer is not an easy task. Traditional media like hoardings, glow signs, flex banners, newspapers and local cable channels etc. are fast losing their effectiveness. The need of the hour is a completely different concept, which is both 21st century and cost effective.

Electronic Display

Electronic display brings you a new-world outdoor media vehicle that arrives on the communication scene like a breath of fresh air. Screen is a 13'x10' electronic, moving display, in bright red colour. The display scrolls continuously and by its sheer movement catches the eye of passers-by. What's more? Your ad will appear repetitively for at least 60 to 70 times a day. Plus, you have the flexibility to change your message on various occasions. What more do you want?

location

The Electronic Display is located at Shivaji Chowk, Kings Corner, Osmanabad. This is the perfect location that sees a lot of shop bound crowd during the day. The best part is that it is visible from both sides of the road. The daily commuters total upto 50,000 people. Needless to say, people who wait at the signal are your captive audience. And of course, there is no way they can switch channels or skip the page.

Electronic Display

The basic advantage in using the Electronic Display is its tremendous reach and potential to generate a huge customer base. All brands, local, national, corporate as well as service industries can benefit immensely from this display. Banks, financial institutions, co-op credit societies, established companies, factories, distributors, dealers, showrooms, consumer / goods manufacturers, readymade garment manufacturers, traders, sellers, educational institutions, coaching classes, wedding halls & lawns, hotels, hospitals, newspaper, travel companies can use the digital display to their advantage.
